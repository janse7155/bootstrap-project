Sean McLoughlin

Jacksepticeye

Sean McLoughlin, also known by his popular YouTube channel "Jacksepticeye", is a 32 year old Irish Youtuber with almost 30 million subscribers. He has been on YouTube for over 10 years. McLoughlin also the co-founder of the clothing brand Cloak, as well as the founder of the coffee company Top of the Morning Coffee.

Cloak Brand is a clothing brand founded by Youtubers Sean McLoughlin and Mark Fischbach, also known as Jacksepticeye and Markiplier. The two work to offer high quality, comfortable clothing for a good price.
