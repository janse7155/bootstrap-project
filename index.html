Seán McLoughlin

"Jacksepticeye"

Seán McLoughlin, also known by his popular YouTube channel "Jacksepticeye", is a 32 year old Irish Youtuber with almost 30 million subscribers. He has been on YouTube for over 10 years. McLoughlin also the co-founder of the clothing brand Cloak, as well as the founder of the coffee company Top of the Mornin Coffee.

Cloak Brand is a clothing brand founded by Youtubers Seán McLoughlin and Mark Fischbach, also known as Jacksepticeye and Markiplier. The company was established in 2018, and the two work to offer high quality, comfortable clothing for a good price.

Top of the Mornin Coffee is a business venture founded by Seán McLoughlin due to his love of coffee. Established in June of 2020, McLoughlin has strived to deliver high quality coffee, tea, hot cocoa, and mugs to any who share in his love of coffee, or who simply want to help fund the business.
